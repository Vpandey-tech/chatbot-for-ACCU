About MechAssist

Our Mission

MechAssist was created to provide mechanical engineers, students, and hobbyists with accessible AI assistance for technical problems. We believe in democratizing access to engineering knowledge through open-source technology.

By leveraging lightweight language models optimized for mechanical engineering domains, we offer a practical alternative to large commercial AI services without compromising on domain-specific knowledge.

Technical Details

  • Model: Based on TinyLlama, fine-tuned for engineering knowledge
  • Backend: Flask RESTful API with Hugging Face Transformers
  • Response Time: Optimized for balance between accuracy and speed
  • Domains: 8 specialized mechanical engineering domains
  • Dependencies: Python, PyTorch, Transformers, Flask
